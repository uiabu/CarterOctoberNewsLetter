Florida & National News Highlights

  • New Florida DUI & Crash Laws (Effective Oct. 1st)

    Harsher penalties now apply for repeat DUI offenses and leaving the scene of a crash. Courts may also order restitution for property damage. Stay safe and informed on the road.

  • Open Carry Gun Ruling Creates Confusion

    A Florida appeals court struck down parts of the open-carry ban, leaving law enforcement and residents uncertain about what’s legal. Be cautious and aware of changing firearm regulations.

  • Immigration Enforcement in Florida

    More than 6,000 arrests have been made under Florida’s immigration enforcement efforts, the highest in the nation. Community leaders urge residents to know their rights.

  • Tropical Storm Preparedness

    The National Weather Service warns of heightened storm activity this month. Stock your emergency kits and plan evacuation routes in case of severe weather.

  • National Consumer Safety Alert

    The FDA has issued recalls on several household and food products this fall. Review the latest announcements to ensure your home remains safe.

🍂 Recipe of the Month: Pumpkin Spice Muffins

A seasonal favorite fluffy muffins with warm pumpkin spice flavors, perfect for cozy fall mornings.

    Ingredients (makes ~12 muffins):

  • 1 3⁄4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp pumpkin spice
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup pumpkin purée
  • 1⁄2 cup brown sugar
  • 1⁄2 cup white sugar
  • 1⁄2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    Instructions (Quick & Easy):

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 F (175°C) and line a muffin tin.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Mix pumpkin, sugars, oil, eggs, and vanilla in another.
  4. Combine wet and dry mixtures until just blended.
  5. Fill muffin cups 3⁄4 full and bake 20 22 minutes.

A wholesome breakfast or snack that brings the taste of fall to your table!

🍹Cocktail of the Month: Autumn Apple Cider Sangria

A refreshing fall drink with crisp apple flavors and a hint of spice perfect for gatherings.

    Ingredients (serves 4 6):

  • 1 bottle w hite wine (Pinot Grigio or Chardonnay)
  • 2 cups apple cider
  • 1⁄2 cup brandy
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 2 apples, thinly sliced
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 2 cinnamon sticks

    Instructions (Quick & Easy):

  1. In a large pitcher, mix wine, cider, brandy, and maple syrup.
  2. Add sliced fruit and cinnamon sticks.
  3. Chill for at least 1 hour to let flavors blend.
  4. Serve over ice and enjoy!
